Help & Support

Getting Started

Welcome to LS DOCS! Follow these simple steps to begin your journey:

  • Visit the homepage and click Sign Up.
  • Fill in your details, including name, email, and password.
  • Verify your email by clicking the confirmation link.
  • Log in and access your personalized dashboard!

Using LS DOCS

Explore powerful tools designed for productivity and collaboration:

  • Create documents effortlessly with the New Document feature.
  • Choose from a wide range of templates for reports, resumes, and more.
  • Share files with your team, assign roles, and collaborate in real time.
  • Track recent activity and access quick actions directly from your dashboard.

Advanced Features

Maximize efficiency with these advanced tools:

  • Version Control: Access previous versions of your documents with a single click.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Chat with team members and leave comments directly in the document.
  • File Conversion: Export your files in PDF, DOCX, or TXT formats seamlessly.

Account Management

Take control of your account with these settings:

  • Update your profile details, such as name and email.
  • Reset your password securely through the Forgot Password option.
  • Manage your subscription plan under Billing.

Troubleshooting

Resolve common issues quickly:

  • Document Not Saving: Check your internet connection and ensure you have available storage.
  • File Upload Errors: Confirm the file format is supported and clear your browser cache.
  • Collaboration Issues: Verify permissions and ensure all shared users have accepted the invitation.

Contact Us

We're here to help! Reach out to us through the following channels:

  • Email: support@lsdocs.com
  • Live Chat: Available during business hours.
  • Help Center: Access tutorials and guides in our knowledge base.
